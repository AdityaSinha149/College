Grammar:
========
E  → TE'
E' → +TE' | ε
T  → FT'
T' → *FT' | ε
F  → (E) | i


FIRST Sets:
===========
FIRST(E)  = {(, i}
FIRST(E') = {+, ε}
FIRST(T)  = {(, i}
FIRST(T') = {*, ε}
FIRST(F)  = {(, i}


FOLLOW Sets:
============
FOLLOW(E)  = {), $}
FOLLOW(E') = {), $}
FOLLOW(T)  = {+, ), $}
FOLLOW(T') = {+, ), $}
FOLLOW(F)  = {*, +, ), $}
